Graphic Designer

As a Graphic Designer, my main responsibility is to create visually compelling and communicative designs for various purposes such as promotions, branding, social media, and other corporate needs. I design logos, brochures, posters, banners, and other visual content using design tools such as Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, and Figma. Video Editor

As a Video Editor, I play a crucial role in processing and assembling video footage into polished, engaging, and informative final products. I edit videos for promotional campaigns, social media content, documentation, and other digital production needs using software like Adobe Premiere Pro, After Effects, or DaVinci Resolve. My responsibilities include cutting clips, adding visual effects, transitions, audio adjustments, and rendering high-quality outputs. Creativity and attention to detail are essential to ensure each video delivers strong visual impact.

As a Social Media Specialist, I am responsible for developing content strategies, managing social media accounts, and increasing engagement and brand visibility across platforms such as Instagram, Facebook, TikTok, and LinkedIn. My duties include planning content calendars, writing compelling captions, using relevant hashtags, analyzing post performance, and fostering audience interaction. I also collaborate with the creative team to ensure that every published content supports marketing goals and significantly enhances brand awareness.
